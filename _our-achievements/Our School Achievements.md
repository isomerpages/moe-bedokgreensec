<p>The Green Audit award is an annual environmental audit programme organised
by the Singapore Environmental Council (SEC).</p>
<p>The programme requires schools to obtain information such as its usage
of electricity, the amount and type of rubbish generated in different areas
in the school and green activities organised by the school.</p>
